Chilli Con Carne

Description

This great chilli recipe has to be one of the best dishes to serve to friends for a casual get-together. An easy sharing favourite that uses up storecupboard ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp oil of choice
  • 1 tsp hot chilli powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 500g lean minced beef
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 tsp dried marjoram
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsbp tomato puree
  • 410g can red kidney beans
  • plain boiled long grain rice, to serve

Directions

  1. Dice onion, chop red bell pepper, and finely chop garlic
  2. Cook onion in pan until slightly translucent
  3. Add garlic, red bell pepper, chilli powder, paprika, and ground cumin; stir well
  4. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally
  5. Add beef to pan and cook until browned
  6. In a separate bowl, crumble beef stock cube into 300ml hot water; add to beef mixture
  7. Add can of chopped tomatoes, dried marjoram, sugar, tomato puree, and add salt and pepper to taste; stir well
  8. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and add a lid; simmer for 20 minutes
  9. Drain and rinse beans, then add to pan; remove lid and return to boil for 10 minutes, adding water if needed
  10. Remove from heat and let sit for 10 minutes
  11. Serve over rice with sour cream
