Chicken Biryani

Description

A great one-pot rice dish that still tastes great a few days later - perfect for leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 300g basmati rice
  • 25g butter
  • 1 large onion, finely slices
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 3 cardamom pods
  • 1 small cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 4 skinless chicken breasts, cut into large chunks
  • 4 tbsp balti curry paste
  • 85g raisins
  • 850ml chicken stock
  • 15g chopped coriander
  • 15g coriander leaves
  • 2 tbsp toasted flaked almonds

Directions

  1. Soak rice in warm water, then wash in cold until water runs clear
  2. Heat butter in saucepan and cook onion with bay leaf, cardamom pods, and cinnamon stick for 10 minutes
  3. Sprinkle in turmeric, then add chicken breasts and curry paste; cook until aromatic
  4. Stir in rice and raisins, then add chicken stock
  5. Place a tight-fitting lid on the pan and bring to a hard boil; lower heat to a minimum and cook for another five minutes
  6. Turn off heat and leave for 10 minutes; add chopped coriander and stir well
  7. Service with coriander leaves amd toasted almonds
