Burrito Casserole

Description

Satisfy your craving for something different tonight. It's good and fairly easy. This is stuff I usually have on hand too.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  • 1/2 medium yellow onion, chopped
  • 1 package taco seasoning
  • 6 large flour tortillas
  • 1 can refried beans
  • 2-3 cups shredded taco cheese
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 4 ounces sour cream

Directions

  1. Brown meat and onion, drain
  2. Add taco seasoning and stir in beans
  3. Mix soup and sour cream in separate bowl
  4. Spread half of sour cream mixture into bottom of casserole dish
  5. Tear up three tortillas and spread over sour cream mixture
  6. Add half the meat and bean mixture
  7. Add a layer of cheese
  8. Repeat layers
  9. Sprinkle cheese on top, bake uncovered at 350Â°F for 20-30 minutes
